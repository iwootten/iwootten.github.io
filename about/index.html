Hi, I’m Ian.

I’m a software engineer living in Cardiff in the UK. I run a small software development company, Nifty Digits Ltd which specialises in Python.

Here I publish my thoughts on anything that takes my fancy - and I have done so for the last 18 years! A lot has changed in that time for me.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Cardiff University. Probably the most valuable skills I learnt whilst writing it were about myself, rather than anything in my final thesis.

I’d love to heard from you if you found anything useful or want to discuss further. The quickest way to get in touch would be via twitter or alternatively any of the other services I use in the sidebar which I check in on less frequently.
