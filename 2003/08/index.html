Upgrade Joys

31 Aug 2003

In a bid to pummel my friends workmates at a bit of LAN gaming yesterday, I took the initiative and upgraded my computer this week. Placed an order on Monday, ordering just the CPU fan from a different store – which proved to be a bad idea, it still hasn’t turned up yet! – preventing me from installing the CPU on my new motherboard…..Tut Tut ebuyer..

All well, its nice to have shedloads of space on my machine now, even if I don’t have any real need for it. This LAN party opened my eyes a bit to the other types of computer geeks out there – pretty much those who just download pirate movies, music, games and porn in their spare time, with enough space to hold it all.

Well, now I look forward to getting a fan, and gradually transferring everything from one hard drive to the next.
