All change

17 Aug 2003

Due to MT being incompatible with my Uni server from an imaging point of view, and the fact that its all coded in Perl – which I haven’t bothered looking at for a hell of a long time, I’ve ditched it and shifted my blog over to b2. Now I can actually post images! Woohoo! b2 is nice and tiny also, a mere 527Kb compared to MT’s 3Mb.

I’m actually toying with the idea of developing a blogging tool as a final year project, I think that might be very rewarding. Whether or not it will happen is another matter.

Blog Install

14 Aug 2003

So, I’ve installed Movable Type on my website. I figured it was about time that I get up to speed on this whole “Blogging” thing, but I could of even missed the bus already.

I’ve spent half of the evening just looking at what can be done with MT, and it seems to integrate well with Invision Board as the MT crew have an example of a forum set up with it on their site. On the other hand it could just be a good hack.

You might wonder why I’ve chosen MT over Drupal. Basically its just aesthetics – I think that MT looks very pretty compared with Drupal, and it seems to be pretty well supported to boot.
