Papers

02 Feb 2006

I’ve just been informed that my paper for CCGRID06 has been accepted, which I’m very pleased about. It’s my first paper acceptance in my 14 months of being based as a PhD at Cardiff University so really its about time I got some ideas out in the open to people. It’s also based in Singapore, and I’ve never left Europe – yeah it’s two days, but I’m chuffed nonetheless.

The ironic thing is I was a bit annoyed last year at the fact CCGRID05 was in Cardiff. I suppose its better this way round.
